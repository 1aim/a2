                            -> FutureReceiver<Result<SignedJws, ()>>

Sign a JWS and return to future resolving to the signature.

If the prot_header parameter does not already contain a alg parameter, that is automatically inserted.

Parameters:

  • prot_header: The JWS protected header. Must be a dictionary.
  • payload: The payload to sign.
  • keypair: The keypair to sign with.
  • algorithm: The algorithm to use. One of the SIG_* constants (TLS signature scheme numbers).

Return value:

  • Future resolving to the signature when available.
